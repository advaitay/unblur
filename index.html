Clarity fades.
This is how you keep it.

Most students lose their clarity within 3 months — not because they're weak, but because the pressure around them gets louder than the voice inside. The ₹499 plan exists for the version of you that doesn't want that to happen.

Simran. 17. Delhi. PCM.

She wasn't bad at physics. She just didn't care about it.

Everyone around her was doing JEE prep. Her parents had already mentally enrolled her in engineering. Her friends were buying HC Verma.

But every time she sat down to study, she'd end up on Pinterest saving brand logos and packaging designs.

She took Unblur on a Tuesday night, mostly out of curiosity. Her archetype was The Creative Strategist. Her conventional path pressure was 84%.

The report said: "You already know what you want. You've just been talked out of wanting it."

She switched to Humanities the following month. Her parents came around by December.

Six months later she came back for the ₹499 plan. Not because she was lost — but because she wanted someone who knew her story to confirm she was still on the right track.

That call lasted 34 minutes. She cried twice. Both times from relief.
